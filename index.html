The Venue

Villa Tantafera is a stunning Tuscan villa dating back to the 15th century. Its hilltop position ensures stunning views of Florence and the Tuscan countryside, with the city center just a 10 minutes drive away.

The Villa is nestled on top of 5 acres of private land, scattered with olive trees. All around the house, a romantic, terraced garden with ponds and fountains, lemon plants and persimmon trees, strawberry grape vine and delicate flowers.

Florence is a beautiful city full of romance and history, and we can't imagine a better place for our friends and family to come together and celebrate with us.
