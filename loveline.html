Loveline

A look back at our years together.

2013
July 27

The day we met

I ordered a pilsner at the bar, and that's when Josef decided to keep me around.

Thanks for inviting all your coworkers to your birthday party, Monica!

2014
June 12

Our first holiday

Less than a year after we first met, we flew to Prague to celebrate Josef's graduation and spend time with his family. Then we did a week of traveling - espressos in Vienna, paella in Barcelona, and homemade sangria in Mallorca!

2015
June 15

Moving in together

We first moved into the master bedroom of the apartment Josef shared with his roommates in January. It wasn't until June that we finally moved into our own one-bedroom apartment in Twin Peaks!

December 12

The Proposal

We were visiting Josef's family for Christmas, so we held our own mini-Christmas the weekend before we left.

Josef wrapped each of my Christmas pesents with a small drawing and meaning behind the gift. The last gift was a beautiful ring that I love almost as much as I love him.

2017
June 3

The Wedding

We're so excited to start the next phase of our lives together and can't wait to celebrate our wedding with everyone in June!
